chefly

personalized meal planner

chefly aims to limit the grocery trips by allowing users to plan their weekly meals from their saved recipes and create a weekly shopping list accordingly. our app also allows users to store their recipes and organize them into personalized folders. lastly, the app provides users with a fun relief from the ongoing pandemic with creative cooking challenges.

for this project we used flutter for the frontend and firebase for the backend.

app interface

this project was one of the first software projects i contributed to and was where i first learned about my interest in ui and design. i fell into the frontend developer role and experienced a steep learning curve with flutter as well as the intricacies of application design. while this is not my best work, it sparked my enthusiasm for design and development!
