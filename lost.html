"Confusing Existance"

The story behind the pieces

I had quite a few themes I wanted to incorporate in my work for this project, but chose to go with the concept of our confusing existence.

creative process

Many times we think who we are and what we do matters so much but in reality we are all collectively doing random things to fill time in our short lifespan. Once I narrowed down on this idea, I began to create.

To mirror the confused bunch of characters, I personify the earth as also being a confused rock just floating around. It is surrounded by planets and stars. If you potentially zoom into their expressions, they are equally lost and confused. This further exemplifies the idea on a larger scale.
