ntr

an innovative job application management and resume parsing platform.

ntr is an innovative job application management and resume parsing platform. instead of spending time filling out applications and neglecting interview practice, you can utilize ntr to do it for you, thereby saving time and effort. ntr efficiently scrapes your resume, retrieves the necessary information, and stores it, automatically filling in job applications as needed. additionally, it aids in tracking your applications, building a profile website, and hosting it.

for this project, we utilized the mern stack (mongodb, express, react, node).

creative process

as the lead front-end developer, i took on the challenge of crafting a compelling brand identity for our application. that started with conceptualizing logos and designing ui mockups

logo design

the main idea I wanted to convey in our logo was seamless completion of job applications with a single click of the enter button. thus I made the decision to incorporate the arrow of the enter key as the primary element of our logo design.
