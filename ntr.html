ntr

An innovative job application management and resume parsing platform.

Instead of wasting time filling out applications and not practicing for interviews, you can use ntr to do it for you, saving time and effort. ntr will scrape your resume, get the information and store it, filling in job applications whenever you need. It will also help track your applications, build a profile website and host it and allow reviews of companies differentiating us from LinkedIn and Glassdoor.

For this project we used the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node).

creative process

As the lead Front-End Developer, I took on the challenge of crafting a compelling brand identity for our application. That started with conceptualizing logos and designing UI Mockups

Logo design

The main idea I wanted to convey in our logo was seamless completion of job applications with a single click of the enter button. Thus I made the decision to incorporate the arrow of the enter key as the primary element of our logo design.
